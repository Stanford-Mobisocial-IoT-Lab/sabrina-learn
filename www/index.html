Train Sabrina

Thanks for being part of the Sabrina virtual assistant project. In here you can help us train her to understand better and more complex sentences.

You can type a sentence in the box below, and Sabrina will respond with her best guess of what you mean. Unfortunately, she will not get it right most of the time, so your task is to then click on the correct interpretation in her pseudo-English.

You don't have to worry, nothing will happen as a result of playing with Sabrina here, and if you don't understand what Sabrina is responding for a particular sentence (or all Sabrina offers are wrong), just type a new sentence to continue.

If you want to know more, check out our website.
